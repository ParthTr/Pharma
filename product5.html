Himalaya Liv.52 DS Tablet
Manufactured By Himalaya Drug Company
Highlights

  • This tablet protects the liver from hepatotoxins.
  • Protects from alcohol-induced liver damage and arrests the progress of cirrhosis.
  • Prevents fatty infiltration of the liver due to chronic alcohol consumption.
  • It is vital for regulating important functions in the body.
  • Improves the appetite and promotes weight gain.
Description
Himalaya Liv.52 DS Tablet restores the function of the liver by protecting the hepatic parenchyma and promoting hepatocellular regeneration. It helps in the removal of acetaldehyde produced by the oxidation of ethanol and protects from alcohol-induced liver damage. It also improves the appetite and promotes weight gain by normalizing the basic appetite-satiety rhythm.
Ingredients
Liv 52 DS Tabletcontains Achillea Millefolium, Cichorium Intybus, Himsra, Jhavuka, Kakamachi, Kasamarda, Mandur Bhasma and Terminalia Arjuna as active ingredients.
Direction of Use
As directed by the doctor.
Storage
Store at room temperature protected from sunlight, heat, and moisture. Keep away from the reach of children and pets.
