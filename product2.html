Description

Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour is used as an effective smoking cessation aid (helps to quit smoking). It relieves and/or prevents the craving and withdrawal symptoms that may be associated with quitting smoking or chewing tobacco. It works by binding to certain receptors (proteins) in your body and helps to reduce your urge to smoke.

Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour may cause common side effects such as headache, throat irritation, cough, loss of taste, soreness/dryness/burning sensation in your mouth, etc. Consult your doctor if these symptoms persist or worsen.

Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour should be taken as directed by your doctor. Take it at the same time every day to maintain a constant level of this medicine in your body.

Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour should be avoided if you are allergic to it. Inform your doctor if you have a history of diabetes (a condition with high blood sugar levels), hypertension (high blood pressure), seizures (fits) or pre-existing kidney, liver, or heart problems before beginning treatment with this medicine. Close evaluation with respect to your current medicines is necessary to avoid any undesired side effects.

Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour is not recommended in children below 12 years of age. If you are pregnant, or breastfeeding, or are planning a pregnancy, consult your doctor before using this medication.

How long does it take for this medicine to take effect?

Is this a habit forming medicine?
Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour may become habit-forming with prolonged use.
Can this medicine be taken during pregnancy?
Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour may cause severe side effects in your developing foetus and is not recommended during pregnancy unless necessary. If you are pregnant, consult your doctor.

Can this medicine be taken while breast-feeding?
Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour passes into breastmilk and may cause severe side effects in your breastfed infant. This medicine is not recommended during breastfeeding unless necessary. If you are breastfeeding, consult your doctor.

  • Allergy
    Avoid Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour if you are allergic to it. Seek immediate medical attention if you notice any symptoms such as skin rash, itching/swelling (especially of your face/tongue/throat), severe dizziness, breathing difficulty, etc.

Warnings for special population

  • Pregnancy
    Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour may cause severe side effects in your developing foetus and is not recommended during pregnancy unless necessary. If you are pregnant, consult your doctor.

  • Breast-feeding
    Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour passes into breastmilk and may cause severe side effects in your breastfed infant. This medicine is not recommended during breastfeeding unless necessary. If you are breastfeeding, consult your doctor.


Warnings for special population

  • Heart diseases
    Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour should be used with caution if you have pre-existing heart problems such as arrhythmias (irregular heartbeats), myocardial infarction (heart attack), angina (severe chest pain), etc due to the increased risk of worsening your condition. Your heart function needs to be closely monitored.

  • High blood pressure
    Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour may increase your blood pressure and should be used with caution if you have a history of hypertension (high blood pressure). Your blood pressure needs to be closely monitored.

  • Stomach ulcers
    Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour should be used with caution if you have a history of stomach ulcers (painful sores on the inner lining of your stomach) due to the increased risk of worsening your condition.

  • Diabetes
    Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour may affect your blood sugar control and should be used with caution if you have a history of diabetes (a condition with high blood sugar levels). Your blood sugar levels need to be closely monitored and dose adjustments of your anti-diabetic medicines may be required based on your clinical condition.


  • Missed Dose
    Try not to skip a dose of Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour. Take the missed dose as soon as you remember. If it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed one. Do not double the dose to compensate for a missed one.

  • Overdose
    Do not take more than the prescribed dose. Contact your doctor if you suspect an overdose with Nicotex Mint Plus Flavour. Overdose symptoms may include dizziness, nausea, vomiting, weakness, diarrhoea, and increased heart rate.
