Limcee Tablet
Manufactured By Abbott Healthcare Pvt. Ltd.
Contains      Vitamin C
Description

Limcee Tablet is used in the treatment of vitamin C deficiency. It is a vitamin supplement that works by improving vitamin C levels in your body.

Limcee Tablet may cause side effects such as diarrhoea, nausea/vomiting, stomach cramps, headache, etc. Consult your doctor if these symptoms persist or worsen.

Limcee Tablet should be taken exactly as directed by your doctor. Take it at the same time every day to maintain a constant level of this medicine in your body.

Limcee Tablet should be avoided if you are allergic to it. Inform your doctor if you have a history of kidney stones before beginning treatment with this medicine. Close evaluation with respect to your current medicines is necessary to avoid any undesired side effects. If you are pregnant or breastfeeding, or are planning a pregnancy, consult your doctor before taking this medication.

Major & minor side effects for Limcee Tablet
  • Nausea or Vomiting
  • Stomach Cramps
  • Diarrhoea
  • Flushing or redness of skin
  • Headache


Uses Of Limcee Tablet

What is it prescribed for?

  • Nutritional deficiencies

    Limcee Tablet improves vitamin C levels in your body. This medicine is used in the prevention and treatment of vitamin C deficiency. It is also used in the treatment of a severe form of vitamin C deficiency called scurvy (a condition that causes symptoms of bruising, bleeding gums, weakness, fatigue, and skin rash)
Concerns
Commonly asked questions


How long does it take for this medicine to take effect?
The time required by Limcee Tablet to show its effect is not clinically known.

How long do the effects of this medicine last?
The duration for which Limcee Tablet remains effective is not clinically known.

Is it safe to consume alcohol while taking this medicine?
Interaction with alcohol is unknown. It is advisable to consult your doctor before consumption.

Is this a habit forming medicine?
No habit-forming tendency has been reported with Limcee Tablet.
Can this medicine be taken during pregnancy?
Due to a lack of safety and efficacy studies in pregnant women, the risk to your developing foetus due to Limcee Tablet is not known. However, this medicine may be safe to use during pregnancy upon your doctor's recommendation.

Can this medicine be taken while breast-feeding?
Limcee Tablet passes into breastmilk. However, it is not reported to cause side effects in your breastfed infant and may be safe to use during breastfeeding upon your doctor's recommendation.

  • Allergy
    Avoid Limcee Tablet if you are allergic to it. Seek immediate medical attention if you notice any symptoms such as skin rash, itching/swelling (especially of your face/tongue/throat), severe dizziness, breathing difficulty, etc.

  • Hyperoxaluria
    Hyperoxaluria is a condition that occurs when there are high levels of oxalate in your urine. Limcee Tablet is not recommended if you have hyperoxaluria due to the increased risk of severe side effects.

Warnings for special population

  • Pregnancy
    Due to a lack of safety and efficacy studies in pregnant women, the risk to your developing foetus due to Limcee Tablet is not known. However, this medicine may be safe to use during pregnancy upon your doctor's recommendation.

  • Breast-feeding
    Limcee Tablet passes into breastmilk. However, it is not reported to cause side effects in your breastfed infant and may be safe to use during breastfeeding upon your doctor's recommendation.


General warnings

  • G6PD deficiency
    G6PD Deficiency is a genetic condition characterized by the deficiency of glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) enzyme. This causes your red blood cells to break down in response to certain medications, infections, or other stresses. Limcee Tablet should be used with caution if you have this condition due to the increased risk of haemolysis (breakdown of your red blood cells).

  • Smoking
    Smoking cigarettes or the use of tobacco products in other forms may reduce the effectiveness of Limcee Tablet and should be avoided.

  • Kidney stones
    Limcee Tablet can acidify the urine, resulting in the formation of urate, cystine, or oxalate stones in your kidneys. This medicine should be used with caution if you have a history of kidney stones. Clinical monitoring of urine for pH and crystal formation is recommended.

  • Other medicines
    Limcee Tablet may interact with other medicines and may cause undesired side effects. Inform your doctor about your current medicines including any herbs or supplements before taking this medicine.


  • Missed Dose
    Try not to skip a dose of Limcee Tablet. Take the missed dose as soon as you remember. If it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed one. Do not double the dose to compensate for a missed one.

  • Overdose
    Do not take more than the prescribed dose. Contact your doctor if you suspect an overdose with Limcee Tablet.
